Simple and powerful robots for education

For teachers, they make it easy to train the engineers of tomorrow with industrial-grade robots.

Collaborative robots for all your needs

ArmBot robots are 4-axis robotic arms designed to efficiently automate repetitive and low value-added tasks. Compact and intuitive, they can be quickly integrated into any industrial or educational environment. Their highly accessible software and compatibility with numerous accessories enable flexible and scalable automation, as well as effective learning.

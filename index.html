📘 About Us

We are a group of passionate students from Algeria 🇩🇿, designing a robotic arm project 🤖 to help develop the tech ecosystem in our country. Our project aims to combine hardware innovation with AI and robotics to improve education and accessibility to smart systems.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to develop smart robots that assist humans in various fields — from education 👩‍🏫 to industry 🏭 and scientific research 🧪. We aim to make AI and robotics accessible to every ambitious Algerian 🇩🇿 youth looking toward the future.

👩‍💻 Our Team

BoneMind is powered by a passionate group of Algerian students and engineers who specialize in robotics and AI. We work with team spirit 💪, love tackling challenges 🧩, and strive to make a real impact in Algeria’s tech scene 🇩🇿💜.
